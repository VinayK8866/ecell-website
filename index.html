Welcome to E-Cell AUM

The Entrepreneurship Cell was started by the students of Amity University Mumbai under the guidance of Amity Innovation Incubator â€“ Noida to foster Entrepreneurship among the students on Campus. We at the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) are focusing on encouraging students to start their enterprises which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop and aspire to cultivate fundamental skills required for real-world business, like Critical thinking, Team Building and Group Dynamics, Networking with Partners, and understanding the legalities of starting their entrepreneurial journey. Entrepreneurship-Cell (E-Cell) also conducts various inter-school workshops, seminars, and events to induce an entrepreneurial ecosystem and helps in providing a platform for individuals with creative minds and potential to become an entrepreneur in all spheres of life.

Vision

To provide an incubator to all the incubates within the campus with industry connections.Inspire students to play a prominent role in leading entrepreneurial activities in the country and make a significant contribution to the global innovation.Our vision is to help students who have an idea, product and burning desire for their startup.

Mission

To create entrepreneurial thinking and environment mong the students and create business leaders in all spheres of life.Our mission is to help the aspiring entrepreneurs start and grow their businesses by creating a pathway of various opportunities like interactive sessions with professionals,business competitions.
